About Me

Hi! :)
My name is Zahra. I know pronouncing an "h" between a vowel and a consonent can be difficult for some people, especially English speakers. So, feel free to call me "Zara", just like the brand!
Talking about pronunciation and language differences, it might be interesting for you to know that I am a "computational linguist". As the name suggests, a computational linguist works on language, computationally! We teach computers how to learn humans' language which is so fun because first you need to understand how humans process the language and then use this knowledge to teach computers the natural language.
